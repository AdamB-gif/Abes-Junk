What Affects Your Price

Pricing Factors

Volume is the main driver, but a few other things can affect the final number. We always explain these before we start.

Volume & Load Size

The single biggest factor. You pay for the percentage of our truck your items fill — nothing more.

Item Weight

Heavy materials like concrete, brick, or tile can add a small surcharge due to disposal fees. We'll flag this upfront.

Access & Location

Ground-floor and garage pickups are standard. Multiple flights of stairs or very tight access may affect the quote.

Item Type

Most items haul at standard rates. Unusually difficult items (pianos, large safes) may have a small add-on — always disclosed first.

Hazardous Materials

We can't haul paint, chemicals, motor oil, asbestos, or propane tanks. Those require special disposal — we'll point you in the right direction.

Same-Day Scheduling

We don't charge extra for same-day service. If we have availability, we come out at the same price — no rush surcharge.

Our pricing promise: The number we quote on-site is the number you pay. We don't adjust after loading, don't add fuel surcharges, and don't charge for the quote itself. If something changes the price (like an unexpectedly heavy load), we tell you before we start — not after.

Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

How much does junk removal cost in Pasco County?
Jobs in Pasco County typically range from $99 for a single-item pickup to $499–$549 for a full truckload. Most residential jobs fall between $159 and $329. We give a firm, no-obligation quote on-site before any work begins.
Is there a minimum charge?
Yes — our minimum is $99, which covers 1 to 2 small items or a single bulky piece like a mattress or appliance.
Do you charge extra for heavy items like appliances or safes?
Not usually. Heavy items are priced by the space they occupy in the truck. Very unusual items (like a piano or large safe) may have a small surcharge — we always tell you upfront before we start.
Is the quote really free?
Yes. We come out, look at the load, and give you a firm price. No obligation, no service fee, no dispatch charge. If you're happy, we can start immediately.
Are you cheaper than 1-800-GOT-JUNK or College Hunks?
Yes — our pricing typically runs 10–15% below national chains for equivalent loads in Pasco County. We also don't add the hidden $99 dispatch/service fee that College Hunks is known for charging on top of the load price.
Do you charge extra for stairs or difficult access?
In most cases, no. We factor normal access into our quote. If the job involves multiple flights of stairs or an unusually difficult situation, we'll mention it when we give your price — never after the work is done.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due after the job is complete and you're satisfied with the result.
