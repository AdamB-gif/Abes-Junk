What We Handle

Items We Remove from Estates

Whether you're managing a loved one's home or liquidating a property, we clear it all — room by room, top to bottom.

Furniture

Sofas, beds, dressers, tables, chairs, cabinets, and all other household furniture throughout the home.

Clothing & Linens

Wardrobes, closets full of clothing, bedding, towels, curtains, and all fabric items.

Personal Belongings

Books, photo frames, decor, collectibles, knick-knacks, and the lifetime accumulation of personal items.

Kitchen Items

Dishes, cookware, small appliances, pantry goods, and full kitchen contents.

Appliances

Refrigerators, washers, dryers, ovens, microwaves, and other major and minor appliances.

Garage Contents

Tools, equipment, storage bins, old hardware, seasonal items, and years of garage accumulation.

Electronics & Media

TVs, computers, stereos, DVDs, records, books, and other electronics and media collections.

Yard & Shed Items

Outdoor furniture, garden tools, lawn equipment, shed contents, and outdoor clutter.

Miscellaneous Debris

Anything left behind — boxes, bags, odds and ends — we clear the entire property.

Our approach: We actively sort items for donation to local Pasco County charities before anything goes to disposal. Salvageable furniture, clothing, and household goods get a second life wherever possible. We also can't take hazardous materials — call us at (727) 810-4816 with any questions.
