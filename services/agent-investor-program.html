Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a sign-up fee or contract?
No sign-up fee. We work on a simple per-job basis by default — but recurring contracts are available for contractors and property managers who prefer them. Just mention it when you call and we'll set it up.
Do I have to send a minimum number of jobs?
No minimum. We offer priority scheduling and better pricing for professionals who send us regular work — but there's no threshold you have to hit first.
Can I give your number directly to my clients?
Yes — give clients our number and let them know you're a partner. We'll handle the job professionally and can keep you in the loop with a status update if you'd like one.
What areas do you cover for partner jobs?
All of Pasco County including New Port Richey, Wesley Chapel, Zephyrhills, Land O' Lakes, Hudson, Trinity, Holiday, Dade City, and surrounding areas in Hillsborough and Pinellas.
