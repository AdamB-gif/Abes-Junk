Frequently Asked Questions

Can you come before my HOA deadline?
Yes — we prioritize HOA jobs because deadlines are real and fines add up. Call us with your deadline date and we'll schedule accordingly, including same-day when available.
What if I don't know exactly what the HOA wants removed?
Tell us what the violation notice says and we'll advise you on what we can clear. We work with you to make sure the right items are gone so the violation gets resolved properly — not just partially.
Do I need to be home during the cleanup?
Not necessarily. With your permission and clear instructions on what to remove, we can clear designated exterior areas without you present. We'll confirm everything before we leave.
Is there a charge just to get a quote?
No — quotes are always free. We'll tell you upfront what we can take, what we can't, and what the full cost will be before a single item moves.
